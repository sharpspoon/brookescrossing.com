Welcome to Brooke's Crossing.

Brooke's Crossing is one of the newer residential communities in Trussville, located off Highway 11 between Target and downtown. This site collects the HOA information residents use most often without the clutter of the old WordPress setup.

Community Notes

The neighborhood speed limit is 23 mph. Please slow down for the safety of children, walkers, and neighbors throughout the subdivision.

Trash pickup is currently on Tuesdays. Place cans at the street the day before pickup and remove them by noon the following day.

Location

Brooke's Crossing is located off Highway 11 in Trussville, between Target and downtown. The entrance is between Serra Kia and Alabama Ortho Spine & Sports.

Mailing address: P.O. Box 541, Trussville, AL 35173.
