Gate Access

Use the gate system carefully.

Residents must be set up in the gate system to access the neighborhood. Gate fobs are available for $30 each through the HOA.

Getting access

Request gate access codes and fobs by emailing hoa@brookescrossing.com. Keep remotes secure and security codes confidential.

Use your remote near the call box. To use a security code, press # and then enter your code.

Guest entry

Visitors can dial your three-digit directory number or scroll to your name at the call box. The gate system will call your phone from 205-308-2556. Press 9 to open the gate or # to deny access.

Important safety notes

Approach the gates slowly when entering or leaving the neighborhood. Do not tailgate another vehicle through the gate. Even when the gates are already open, slow to nearly a stop so the sensors detect your vehicle.

The HOA is not responsible for gate damage caused by residents or guests. Cameras record inbound and outbound traffic, including license plates.
