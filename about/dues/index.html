Annual Dues

HOA dues are $400 per year.

Annual dues are due on January 1 and become delinquent after January 31.

Payment details

A late fee of $20 per month is assessed on delinquent balances.

Make checks payable to Brooke's Crossing HOA and mail them to P.O. Box 541, Trussville, AL 35173.

Credit card payments

Credit card payments can be processed by calling HOA accountant Pamela Dunn at 205-508-5652 or emailing pamcpa06@gmail.com. A fee applies to credit card payments.
