Architectural Review Committee

Keeping the neighborhood in shape.

The ARC reviews exterior changes, property appearance, parking issues, and general compliance with HOA standards. Contact the HOA before starting any exterior modification.

What the ARC covers

It is important for the neighborhood to maintain its appearance and property standards. ARC review applies to items including lawns, building changes, fences, paint colors, vehicle parking, and trash can storage.

For changes to any existing structure, contact the HOA by email and complete the ARC request form. Complete requests receive an initial review in approximately fifteen business days, although some requests may take longer when research or follow-up is needed.

Parking and trash cans

There is no overnight parking on subdivision streets. Guest parking for social engagements is permitted, and short exceptions for limited overnight guests should be requested through the HOA.

Trash pickup is currently on Tuesdays. Residents should bag trash, place cans on the street the day before pickup, and remove them by noon the next day. Trash cans should be stored on the side or rear of the home, not in front.

Yards and pets

Property owners are responsible for maintaining lawns, landscaping, and trees. Residents are encouraged to keep yards mowed, tidy, and treated as needed.

All residents are required to pick up after their pets.
